Who We Are

This is the official website of BitHead ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we use your data.

What Data We Collect

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymized information such as pages visited, time spent on site, browser/device type, and approximate location. No personally identifiable information is collected by us through Google Analytics.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to remember your cookie consent preferences and to enable Google Analytics only if you consent.
  • Contact Form: If you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message. This information is securely forwarded to us via Formspree and is used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Whitelist Form: If you join our whitelist, we collect your name, email, and Solana wallet address. This is used to manage whitelist access and is stored securely in our backend database.

How We Use Your Data

  • To analyze and improve our website (Google Analytics, only if you consent).
  • To respond to your inquiries (contact form).
  • To manage whitelist access for special features or drops.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics: See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Formspree: Used for secure contact form submissions. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

  • You can decline non-essential cookies at any time using the cookie banner.
  • You may request deletion of your contact or whitelist data by emailing us at bithead.nft@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at bithead.nft@gmail.com.
